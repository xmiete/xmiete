# 🚀 Announcing XMiete Core 1.0: The Open-Source Standard for Digital Rental Deposits

We are proud to announce the initial release of **XMiete Core**, a unified open-source standard designed to digitize the end-to-end lifecycle of rental deposits (Mietkaution) according to BGB § 551.

In an industry fragmented by proprietary silos, XMiete Core bridges the gap between **Fintechs, Banks, Insurers, and Property Management software** through a robust, transparent, and developer-friendly framework.

## 🌟 Key Features
- **Modular JSON Schema**: A flexible standard supporting Cash Deposits, Bank Guarantees, and Insurances.
- **Formal State-Machine**: Clear lifecycle management from `REQUESTED` to `PLEDGED` and `RELEASED`.
- **Financial-Grade Security**: Built-in support for **mTLS**, **OAuth2 scopes**, and **JWS signing** for non-repudiation.
- **Legal Compliance**: Native support for German Tax ID validation and eID (Online-Ausweisfunktion) identity verification.

## 🤝 Stakeholder Ecosystem
XMiete Core is built for the entire market, with architectural support for:
- **Banking Networks**: Sparkassen, Volksbanken (via PlusForta), PSD Banken.
- **Specialized Institutions**: Aareal Bank (GetMomo), Hausbank München eG, DKB, Instabank (Smartmiete).
- **Insurances & Fintechs**: heykaution, getmomo, kautionsfrei.de, and more.
